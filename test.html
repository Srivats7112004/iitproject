❓ FAQ & Methodology

Formulas used, units to enter, and typical values

What should I enter in each field?
  • State of Use: Choose your State/UT. This selects the grid CO₂ factor (g CO₂/kWh) for EVs.
  • Vehicle Type: 2W, 3W, 4W, or Bus. Affects defaults (e.g., bus diesel = 2.6 km/L if not provided).
  • Manufacturing Year: Currently cosmetic unless you wire year-based grid factors.
  • Daily Distance (km): Your average daily driving. The calculator multiplies by 365 to estimate annual.
    Tip: If you’ve switched your code to accept annual distance, just enter annual km instead and ignore “daily”.
  • ICE Efficiency (km/L): Real-world fuel economy of your petrol/diesel vehicle.
  • EV Efficiency (kWh/km): Energy per km. If you have Wh/km, divide by 1000 (e.g., 120 Wh/km → 0.12 kWh/km).
  • Primary Fuel Comparison: Choose Petrol or Diesel for the ICE comparison.
How do you calculate EV emissions?

Per‑km emissions (g CO₂/km):
EV g/km = EV energy (kWh/km) × State EF (g CO₂/kWh)

Annual emissions:
EV g/year = (EV g/km) × Annual Distance (km)
If you entered daily distance: Annual Distance = Daily × 365

How do you calculate Petrol/Diesel emissions?

Fuel CO₂ per km (g CO₂/km):
g/km = (1 / (km/L)) × (Fuel EF g/L) × 1.15

  • Fuel EF (tailpipe): Petrol = 2271 g/L, Diesel = 2653 g/L
  • × 1.15 adds 15% for upstream emissions (refining, transport). Adjust if you prefer a different uplift.
  • Annual emissions: g/year = (g/km) × Annual Distance (km)
  • Bus default: if Vehicle Type = “bus” and no value given, Diesel efficiency defaults to 2.6 km/L.
What grid factor (State EF) do you use?
  • Unit: g CO₂/kWh
  • Source: your sheet column “EF CO2 (kg/kWh) 2024 (new EF/λ)” converted ×1000 to g/kWh.
  • Fallback (India average): ~1327 g/kWh if a state factor is missing.

Example (4‑W in Telangana): EV 0.12 kWh/km → ~166 g/km (State EF ≈ 1385 g/kWh). Petrol 14 km/L → ~187 g/km. EV ≈ 11% lower per‑km.

Common pitfalls
  • Units: EV must be kWh/km (not Wh/km). If you only know Wh/km, divide by 1000.
  • Distance: If your field says “Daily”, the tool multiplies by 365. If you switched to annual in code, enter annual km.
  • Vehicle mismatch: Comparing a diesel bus vs petrol in the selector can give odd results—pick the relevant fuel.
